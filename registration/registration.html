Registration Overview

Everything you need to know about registering for the 3rd RL Bootcamp

Note: Registration details are tentative and subject to change.

The 3rd Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp is open to students, researchers, and professionals interested in reinforcement learning. Registration is completely free of charge thanks to our sponsors, but spaces are limited. Please register early to secure your spot!

Important Dates

Registration opens: June 2026
Registration deadline: August 2026
Bootcamp dates: September 16–18, 2026

Who Can Attend

The bootcamp is designed for participants with basic knowledge of machine learning concepts. No prior experience with reinforcement learning is required.

What to Bring

Participants should bring their own laptop with Python installed. Pre-installation instructions will be sent after registration is confirmed.

Fees & Deadlines

Important information about registration fees and key dates

Registration is Free of Charge!

Thanks to our generous sponsors, there is no registration fee for the 3rd Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp. However, spaces are limited and will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
